Former Deputy President Rigathi Gachagua

Rigathi Gachagua is widely recognized as a leader with an unwavering commitment to integrity, accountability, and national development. Known for his firm stance on economic empowerment and grassroots development, he has emerged as a powerful voice for the Kenyan people. Gachagua’s approach to leadership combines pragmatic solutions with a compassionate understanding of community needs, making him a trusted figure within the political landscape of Kenya. His role as Kenya’s Deputy President has amplified his dedication to promoting transparent governance, sustainable economic growth, and improved welfare for all Kenyans.

Personal Life

Away from politics, Rigathi Gachagua is a family-oriented individual, married to Pastor Dorcas Rigathi, a well-known spiritual leader and community advocate. Together, they share a passion for service and philanthropy, often working hand-in-hand to improve lives and support the vulnerable. Their family is deeply committed to faith, social welfare, and empowering youth and women across Kenya. Gachagua is also an avid supporter of local culture, frequently engaging in community events and fostering unity and pride within his roots in Nyeri County. In his personal life, Gachagua is described as down-to-earth and approachable, traits that resonate with his grassroots support base. His commitment to a better Kenya is evident not only in his political life but also in the personal values and close community bonds he continues to uphold.

Political Career

Rigathi Gachagua’s political career is a testament to his dedication to transparent governance and people-centered leadership. Beginning as a civil servant, Gachagua worked in various governmental roles where he developed a keen understanding of Kenya's administrative landscape. He first served as District Officer and later as a Personal Assistant to the then-Minister for Home Affairs, where he honed his leadership skills and gained valuable insights into public administration.

In 2017, Gachagua made his debut as an elected official, winning the Mathira Constituency parliamentary seat. During his tenure as Member of Parliament, he was instrumental in promoting local development, advocating for policy changes, and addressing community challenges. His exemplary performance as an MP led to his elevation as Deputy President of Kenya, marking a significant milestone in his political journey. In this role, he continues to advocate for accountability, champion reforms, and play a pivotal part in shaping Kenya’s future.

Biography

Rigathi Gachagua is a prominent Kenyan politician and public servant whose career spans decades of dedicated service to his country. Born in 1965 in Mathira, Nyeri County, Gachagua grew up with a keen interest in leadership, community welfare, and governance. He pursued higher education at the University of Nairobi, where he obtained a degree in Political Science and Literature. With a deep-rooted commitment to uplifting communities and promoting fair governance, he has held various roles within the public and private sectors, showcasing his expertise and passion for effective administration and public service.
